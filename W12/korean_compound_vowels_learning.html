Korean Learning: Compound Vowels

Welcome to Korean Learning!

This lesson focuses on Korean compound vowels with ㅔ and ㅐ sounds.

이 수업은 ㅔ와 ㅐ 소리 복합 모음을 학습합니다.

게
ge
Similar to: "get" (in English)
게장
ge-jang - marinated crab
간장 게장은 밥도둑이에요
gan-jang ge-jang-eun bap-do-duk-i-e-yo - Ganjang Gejang is a dish that steals the rice (because it's so delicious)
게는 옆으로 걸어요
ge-neun yeop-eu-ro geo-reo-yo - Crabs walk sideways
네
ne
Similar to: "net" (in English)
네모
ne-mo - square
아이는 종이에 커다란 네모를 그렸습니다
a-i-neun jong-i-e keo-da-ran ne-mo-reul geu-ryeot-seum-ni-da - The child drew a large square on the paper
네, 알겠습니다
ne, al-get-seum-ni-da - Yes, I understand
데
de
Similar to: "damage" (in English)
데미안
de-mi-an - Demian (novel by Hermann Hesse)
소설 데미안은 어려운 책입니다
so-seol de-mi-an-eun eo-ryeo-un chaek-im-ni-da - The novel Demian is a challenging read
어디 가는 데 도움이 필요하세요?
eo-di ga-neun de do-um-i pi-ryo-ha-se-yo? - Do you need help getting somewhere?
레
re / le
Similar to: "lemon" (in English)
레몬
re-mon - lemon
여름철 레몬 에이드는 찐입니다
yeo-reum-cheol re-mon e-i-deu-neun jjin-im-ni-da - Lemonade in the summer is the real deal
레스토랑에서 저녁을 먹었어요
re-seu-to-rang-e-seo jeo-nyeok-eul meok-eoss-eo-yo - I ate dinner at a restaurant
메
me
Similar to: "merry" (in English)
메뚜기
me-ttu-gi - grasshopper
논에 강충강충 뛰어다니는 메뚜기가 있어요
non-e gang-chung-gang-chung ttwi-eo-da-ni-neun me-ttu-gi-ga iss-eo-yo - There are grasshoppers hopping around in the rice paddy
메뉴판을 보여 주세요
me-nyu-pan-eul bo-yeo ju-se-yo - Please show me the menu
베
be
Similar to: "best" (in English)
베짱이
be-jjang-i - cicada
베짱이는 여름 내내 노래만 불렀습니다
be-jjang-i-neun yeo-reum nae-nae no-rae-man bul-reot-seum-ni-da - The cicada sang all summer long
배가 고파서 빵을 샀어요
bae-ga go-pa-seo ppang-eul sass-eo-yo - I was hungry, so I bought bread
세
se
Similar to: "separate" (in English)
세월
se-wol - time, years
노인의 깊은 주름은 세월의 흔적이다
no-in-ui gi-peun ju-reum-eun se-wol-ui heun-jeok-i-da - The deep wrinkles on the old man are the marks of time
세 명의 친구가 함께 여행했어요
se myeong-ui chin-gu-ga ham-kke yeo-haeng-haess-eo-yo - Three friends traveled together
에
e
Similar to: "Esther" (in English)
에어컨
e-eo-keon - air conditioner
여름인데 에어컨이 고장났다
yeo-reum-in-de e-eo-keon-i go-jang-nat-da - It's summer, and the air conditioner is broken
학교에 가는 길에 친구를 만났어요
hak-gyo-e ga-neun gil-e chin-gu-reul man-nass-eo-yo - I met a friend on the way to school
제
je
Similar to: "gesture" (in English)
제비
je-bi - swallow (bird)
강남에는 제비가 많다
gang-nam-e-neun je-bi-ga man-ta - There are many swallows in Gangnam
제 이름은 김민수입니다
je i-reum-eun gim-min-su-im-ni-da - My name is Kim Minsu
체
che
Similar to: "chess" (in English)
체스
che-seu - chess
형은 체스를 잘 둔다
hyeong-eun che-seu-reul jal dun-da - My older brother plays chess well
운동으로 체력을 기르세요
un-dong-eu-ro che-ryeok-eul gi-reu-se-yo - Build your stamina through exercise
케
ke
Similar to: "kept" or "captive" (in English)
케이크
ke-i-keu - cake
누나는 치즈 케이크를 좋아한다
nu-na-neun chi-jeu ke-i-keu-reul jo-a-han-da - My older sister likes cheesecake
케이블카를 타고 산 정상에 올라갔어요
ke-i-beul-ka-reul ta-go san jeong-sang-e ol-ra-gass-eo-yo - I took the cable car up to the mountain peak
테
te
Similar to: "terrace" (in English)
테이블
te-i-beul - table
테이블 매너도 중요하다
te-i-beul mae-neo-do jung-yo-ha-da - Table manners are also important
테니스를 치러 갈까요?
te-ni-seu-reul chi-reo gal-kka-yo? - Shall we go play tennis?
페
pe
Similar to: "paint" (in English)
페인트
pe-in-teu - paint
루미는 지붕을 분홍색 페인트로 칠했다
ru-mi-neun ji-bung-eul bun-hong-saek pe-in-teu-ro chil-haet-da - Lumi painted the roof with pink paint
이 책은 페이지가 많아요
i chaek-eun pe-i-ji-ga man-a-yo - This book has many pages
헤
he
Similar to: "heritage" (in English)
헤어지다
he-eo-ji-da - to break up, to leave
'헤어질 결심'은 좋은 영화다
'he-eo-jil gyeol-sim'-eun jo-eun yeong-hwa-da - 'Decision to Leave' is a good movie
물속에서 헤엄치는 것을 좋아해요
mul-sok-e-seo he-eom-chi-neun geot-eul jo-a-hae-yo - I like swimming in the water
