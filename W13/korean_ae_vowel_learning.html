한국어 학습: ㅐ 발음
Korean Learning: ㅐ Sound

Welcome to Korean Learning!

This lesson focuses on Korean words with ㅐ sound.

이 수업은 ㅐ 소리를 학습합니다.

개
Gae
Similar to: "Get" (in English)
개나리
gae-na-ri - Forsythia
봄이 오면 개나리가 노란 꽃을 피웁니다.
bom-i o-myeon gae-na-ri-ga no-ran kkot-eul pi-um-ni-da - When spring comes, the forsythia blooms with yellow flowers.
개는 사람의 가장 좋은 친구입니다.
gae-neun sa-ram-ui ga-jang jo-eun chin-gu-im-ni-da - Dogs are man's best friend.
내
Nae
Similar to: "Net" (in English)
내일
nae-il - Tomorrow
내일 아침 일찍 일어나야 해요.
nae-il a-chim il-jjik il-eo-na-ya hae-yo - I must wake up early tomorrow morning.
이것은 내 책입니다.
i-geot-eun nae chaek-im-ni-da - This is my book.
대
Dae
Similar to: "Damage" (in English)
대학교
dae-hak-gyo - University
그는 꿈을 이루기 위해 대학교에 진학했어요.
geu-neun kkum-eul i-ru-gi wi-hae dae-hak-gyo-e jin-hak-haess-eo-yo - He went on to a university to achieve his dream.
대나무는 매우 튼튼합니다.
dae-na-mu-neun mae-u teun-teun-ham-ni-da - Bamboo is very strong.
래
Lae
Similar to: "Lemon" (in English)
래퍼
lae-peo - Rapper
그는 유명한 래퍼입니다.
geu-neun yu-myeong-han lae-peo-im-ni-da - He is a famous rapper.
래핑은 리듬감이 중요합니다.
lae-ping-eun ri-deum-gam-i jung-yo-ham-ni-da - Rhythm is important in rapping.
매
Mae
Similar to: "Merry" (in English)
매미
mae-mi - Cicadas
매미가 시끄럽게 울어요.
mae-mi-ga si-kkeu-reop-ge ul-eo-yo - Cicadas chirp loudly.
매일 운동을 합니다.
mae-il un-dong-eul ham-ni-da - I exercise every day.
배
Bae
Similar to: "Best" (in English)
배
bae - Ship / Pear / Stomach
나는 배를 타고 여행하고 싶어요.
na-neun bae-reul ta-go yeo-haeng-ha-go si-peo-yo - I want to go on a trip by boat.
한국 배는 아주 달고 맛있어요.
han-guk bae-neun a-ju dal-go ma-sis-seo-yo - Korean pears are very sweet and delicious.
새
Sae
Similar to: "Separate" (in English)
새우
sae-u - Shrimp
새우는 등이 굽었어요.
sae-u-neun deung-i gup-eoss-eo-yo - Shrimp have bent backs.
하늘에 새가 날아다닙니다.
ha-neul-e sae-ga nal-a-da-nim-ni-da - Birds fly in the sky.
애
Ae
Similar to: "Esther" (in English)
애벌레
ae-beol-le - Caterpillar
애벌레가 나뭇잎을 먹고 있어요.
ae-beol-le-ga na-mut-ip-eul meok-go iss-eo-yo - The caterpillar is eating a leaf.
애기가 울고 있어요.
ae-gi-ga ul-go iss-eo-yo - The baby is crying.
재
Jae
Similar to: "Jesture" (in English)
재산
jae-san - Wealth / Assets
그는 많은 재산을 모았습니다.
geu-neun man-eun jae-san-eul mo-ass-eum-ni-da - He accumulated a lot of wealth (or assets).
그녀는 재능이 많아요.
geu-nyeo-neun jae-neung-i man-a-yo - She has many talents.
채
Chae
Similar to: "Chess" (in English)
채소
chae-so - Vegetables
건강을 위해 채소를 많이 드세요.
geon-gang-eul wi-hae chae-so-reul man-i deu-se-yo - Please eat a lot of vegetables for your health.
냉장고에 신선한 채소가 있어요.
naeng-jang-go-e sin-seon-han chae-so-ga iss-eo-yo - There are fresh vegetables in the refrigerator.
캐
Kae
Similar to: "Kept / Captive" (in English)
캐릭터
kae-rik-teo - Character
제가 가장 좋아하는 만화 캐릭터예요.
je-ga ga-jang jo-a-ha-neun man-hwa kae-rik-teo-ye-yo - That is my favorite cartoon character.
캐나다는 큰 나라입니다.
kae-na-da-neun keun na-ra-im-ni-da - Canada is a big country.
태
Tae
Similar to: "Terrace" (in English)
태양
tae-yang - Sun
태양이 하늘에서 빛나고 있습니다.
tae-yang-i ha-neul-e-seo bit-na-go iss-eum-ni-da - The sun is shining in the sky.
태풍이 오고 있어요.
tae-pung-i o-go iss-eo-yo - A typhoon is coming.
패
Pae
Similar to: "Paint" (in English)
패자
pae-ja - Loser
패자는 다음 기회를 준비합니다.
pae-ja-neun da-eum gi-hoe-reul jun-bi-ham-ni-da - The loser prepares for the next opportunity.
그는 패션에 관심이 많아요.
geu-neun pae-syeon-e gwan-sim-i man-a-yo - He is very interested in fashion.
해
Hae
Similar to: "Heritage" (in English)
해변
hae-byeon - Beach
우리는 해변에서 수영을 했어요.
u-ri-neun hae-byeon-e-seo su-yeong-eul haess-eo-yo - We swam at the beach.
해가 동쪽에서 떠올라요.
hae-ga dong-jjok-e-seo tteo-ol-la-yo - The sun rises from the east.
