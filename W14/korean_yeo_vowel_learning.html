한국어 학습: ㅕ 발음
Korean Learning: ㅕ Sound

Welcome to Korean Learning!

This lesson focuses on Korean words with ㅕ sound.

이 수업은 ㅕ 소리를 학습합니다.

겨
Gyeo
Similar to: "Gear" (in English)
겨울
gyeo-ul - Winter
겨울에는 눈이 많이 와요.
gyeo-ul-e-neun nun-i man-i wa-yo - It snows a lot in winter.
겨울 방학이 기다려져요.
gyeo-ul bang-hak-i gi-da-ryeo-jyeo-yo - I'm looking forward to winter vacation.
녀
Nyeo
Similar to: "Near" (in English)
남녀
nam-nyeo - Man and Woman
이 공원은 남녀노소 누구나 이용할 수 있습니다.
i gong-won-eun nam-nyeo-no-so nu-gu-na i-yong-hal su iss-eum-ni-da - This park is open to men and women of all ages.
남녀평등은 중요합니다.
nam-nyeo-pyeong-deung-eun jung-yo-ham-ni-da - Gender equality is important.
뎌
Dyeo
Similar to: "Dear" (in English)
더뎌 (더디다)
deo-dyeo (deo-di-da) - Slow
회복 속도가 생각보다 더뎌서 걱정이에요.
hoe-bok sok-do-ga saeng-gak-bo-da deo-dyeo-seo geok-jeong-i-e-yo - I'm worried because the recovery is slower than expected.
프로젝트 진행이 너무 더뎌요.
peu-ro-jek-teu jin-haeng-i neo-mu deo-dyeo-yo - The project progress is too slow.
려
Lyeo
Similar to: "Rear" (in English)
배려
bae-ryeo - Consideration
타인을 향한 작은 배려가 세상을 따뜻하게 만듭니다.
ta-in-eul hyang-han ja-geun bae-ryeo-ga se-sang-eul tta-tteu-ta-ge man-deum-ni-da - Small consideration for others makes the world a warmer place.
상대방을 배려하는 마음이 중요해요.
sang-dae-bang-eul bae-ryeo-ha-neun ma-eum-i jung-yo-hae-yo - A considerate heart for others is important.
며
Myeo
Similar to: "Mere" (in English)
며느리
myeo-neu-ri - Daughter-in-law
우리 할머니는 며느리를 무척 아끼십니다.
u-ri hal-meo-ni-neun myeo-neu-ri-reul mu-cheok a-kki-sim-ni-da - My grandmother cherishes her daughter-in-law very much.
며느리와 시어머니의 관계가 좋아요.
myeo-neu-ri-wa si-eo-meo-ni-ui gwan-gye-ga jo-a-yo - The relationship between daughter-in-law and mother-in-law is good.
벼
Byeo
Similar to: "Beer" (in English)
벼
byeo - Rice Plant
가을이 되면 논에 벼가 황금빛으로 익습니다.
ga-eul-i doe-myeon non-e byeo-ga hwang-geum-bit-eu-ro ik-seum-ni-da - In autumn, the rice plants in the fields ripen into a golden color.
농부들이 벼를 수확하고 있어요.
nong-bu-deul-i byeo-reul su-hwak-ha-go iss-eo-yo - Farmers are harvesting rice plants.
셔
Syeo
Similar to: "Sure" (in English)
셔틀버스
syeo-teul-beo-seu - Shuttle Bus
역 앞에서 매시 정각에 셔틀버스가 출발합니다.
yeok ap-e-seo mae-si jeong-gak-e syeo-teul-beo-seu-ga chul-bal-ham-ni-da - The shuttle bus departs from the front of the station every hour on the dot.
공항까지 셔틀버스를 이용하면 편리해요.
gong-hang-kka-ji syeo-teul-beo-seu-reul i-yong-ha-myeon pyeol-li-hae-yo - It's convenient to use the shuttle bus to the airport.
여
Yeo
Similar to: "Young" (in English)
여우
yeo-u - Fox
여우는 정말 영리한 동물이에요.
yeo-u-neun jeong-mal yeong-ri-han dong-mul-i-e-yo - Foxes are very clever animals.
동화 속에 여우가 자주 등장해요.
dong-hwa sok-e yeo-u-ga ja-ju deung-jang-hae-yo - Foxes often appear in fairy tales.
져
Jyeo
Similar to: "Jersey" (in English)
가져 (가지다)
ga-jyeo (ga-ji-da) - Take / Bring
이 사탕 네가 가져. (You can take this candy.)
i sa-tang ne-ga ga-jyeo - You can take this candy.
우산을 꼭 가져가세요.
u-san-eul kkok ga-jyeo-ga-se-yo - Please make sure to bring an umbrella.
쳐
Chyeo
Similar to: "Cheer" (in English)
고쳐 (고치다)
go-chyeo (go-chi-da) - Fix / Repair
고장 난 컴퓨터를 스스로 고쳐 봤어요. (I tried to fix the broken computer by myself.)
go-jang nan keom-pyu-teo-reul seu-seu-ro go-chyeo bwass-eo-yo - I tried to fix the broken computer by myself.
잘못된 부분을 고쳐 주세요.
jal-mot-doen bu-bun-eul go-chyeo ju-se-yo - Please fix the incorrect parts.
켜
Kyeo
Similar to: "Cure" (in English)
켜다
kyeo-da - Turn On
어두우니 불을 좀 켜다 줄래? (Can you turn on the light? It's dark.)
eo-du-u-ni bul-eul jom kyeo-da jul-lae - Can you turn on the light? It's dark.
텔레비전을 켜서 뉴스를 봐요.
tel-le-bi-jeon-eul kyeo-seo nyu-seu-reul bwa-yo - I turn on the TV and watch the news.
텨
Tyeo
Similar to: "Tear" (in English)
버텨 (버티다)
beo-tyeo (beo-ti-da) - Hold On / Endure
조금 힘들어도 조금만 더 버텨 봐. (Even if it's hard, try to hold on a little longer.)
jo-geum him-deul-eo-do jo-geum-man deo beo-tyeo bwa - Even if it's hard, try to hold on a little longer.
어려운 시기를 잘 버텨냈어요.
eo-ryeo-un si-gi-reul jal beo-tyeo-naess-eo-yo - I endured the difficult period well.
펴
Pyeo
Similar to: "Pure" (in English)
펴다
pyeo-da - Open / Stretch
비가 오니까 우산을 펴다. (Open the umbrella because it's raining.)
bi-ga o-ni-kka u-san-eul pyeo-da - Open the umbrella because it's raining.
책을 펴서 공부해요.
chaek-eul pyeo-seo gong-bu-hae-yo - I open the book and study.
혀
Hyeo
Similar to: "Here" (in English)
혀
hyeo - Tongue
뜨거운 국을 먹다가 혀를 데었어요. (I burnt my tongue while eating hot soup.)
tteu-geo-un guk-eul meok-da-ga hyeo-reul de-eoss-eo-yo - I burnt my tongue while eating hot soup.
발음을 할 때 혀의 위치가 중요해요.
bal-eum-eul hal ttae hyeo-ui wi-chi-ga jung-yo-hae-yo - The position of the tongue is important when pronouncing.
