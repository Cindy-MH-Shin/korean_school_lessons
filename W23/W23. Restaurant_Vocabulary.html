🏮 1단계 · 식당 가기 전 — Preparation
Step 1-A
예약
豫約
Ye-yak
Reservation
豫
예 (ye)
미리 / in advance
🐘
+
約
약 (yak)
맺다 / to bind
🪢
📖 STORY
豫 pictures a wise elephant (象) — known for its extraordinary memory and foresight. 約 shows silk thread being bound into a knot 🪢. Together: "The wise elephant ties a knot ahead of time." → You look ahead and secure your seat before going!
Step 1-B
입구
入口
Ip-gu
Entrance
入
입 (ip)
들다 / to enter
🚶
+
口
구 (gu)
입·구멍 / mouth, opening
🚪
📖 STORY
入 looks like a person tilting forward to duck inside 🚶 — head first, stepping in. 口 is a square mouth / opening. Together: "the opening you walk into." Compare with 출구 出口 — same 口, but 出 (going OUT like a sprout growing upward 🌱).
🍽️ 2단계 · 즐거운 식사 시간 — The Meal
Step 2-A
식사
食事
Sik-sa
A Meal

먹다 / to eat
🍚

사 (sa)

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
📖 STORY
食 depicts a covered pot of rice 🍚 being eaten. 事 means a significant happening or affair. Together: eating is not just a biological act — it is a meaningful family event, a gathering, a ritual worth honoring. That's why Koreans say "식사 맛있게 하세요!" with such warmth.

📖 STORY
飮食 = everything you drink 🥤 AND eat 🍚. A beautifully comprehensive word. Notice that 식사 (食事) focuses on the act of eating as an occasion, while 음식 (飮食) refers to the food itself. Both share 食 — the root of nourishment!
👥 3단계 · 몇 인분? — Counting Portions
Step 3
인분
人份
In-bun
Serving / Portion
人
인 (in)
사람 / person
🧑
+
份
분 (bun)
몫 / one's share
⚖️
📖 STORY — 份의 비밀 The secret inside 份
份 = 亻(person 사람) + 分 · And 分 = 刀(knife 칼) + 八(divide 나누다). So 份 means: a knife cuts food into fair shares for each person ⚖️🔪.

Then 1인분, 2인분, 3인분, 4인분 use Sino-Korean numbers (일·이·삼·사) + 人份. A uniquely Korean way of blending Chinese characters with numbers to count portions!

入口 (ip-gu) = the opening you walk into 🚶→🏠

Same 口, opposite direction! Easy to remember together.

🍽️ 식당 단어 퀴즈

Restaurant Vocabulary Quiz · 10 questions · 정답을 골라보세요!

Q1
예약 의 뜻은?
✅ 예약 (豫約) = Reservation. 豫 (in advance) + 約 (to bind/promise). You tie up a promise ahead of time — just like a wise elephant planning ahead! 🐘
Q2
"4 servings of pork belly, please." 를 한국어로 하면?
✅ 4인분 (사인분) = 4 servings. 人份 = person's fair share. 개 = general counter for things · 명 = counter for people · 인분 = specifically for food portions!
Q3
음식 과 식사 의 차이는?
✅ 음식 (飮食) = the food & drink themselves. 식사 (食事) = the meal as a meaningful occasion / event. Both share 食, but 事 (affair/event) makes 식사 more formal and ceremonial!
Q4
"May I take your order?" 를 한국어로 하면?
✅ 주문 받아도 될까요? = May I take your order? 주문 (注文) = order · 받다 = to receive · ~아도 될까요? = May I...?
Q5
입구 와 출구 의 공통 한자는?
✅ Both 입구(入口) and 출구(出口) share 口 = an opening/mouth. 入 = enter, 出 = exit. Same door 🚪, opposite direction!
Q6
식당에서 음식을 먹기 전에 하는 인사는?
✅ 잘 먹겠습니다 = "I will eat well" (BEFORE eating — future tense 겠). 잘 먹었습니다 = "I ate well" (AFTER eating — past tense 었). The tense tells the whole story!
Q7
추천 (推薦) 에서 推의 뜻은?
✅ 推 = to push 밀다. 薦 = to recommend. 추천 (推薦) = to push something good toward someone — you're literally advocating for it! "Let me push this dish toward you — it's amazing!" 🍛
Q8
"The check, please!" 를 한국어로 하면?
✅ 계산해 주세요 = Please calculate (the bill). 계산서 (計算書) = the written bill/receipt. 저기요! = "Excuse me!" to get the server's attention first!
Q9
份 (분) 의 뜻을 가장 잘 설명한 것은?
✅ 份 = 亻(person) + 分 · and 分 = 刀(knife) + 八(divide). A knife fairly divides food for each person ⚖️🔪. So 3인분 = three people's fair shares. Beautiful!
Q10
다음 중 4인 자리 의 올바른 뜻은?
✅ 4인 자리 = 4人 자리 = a seat/spot for 4 people. 자리 = place, spot, seat. "4인 자리 있어요?" is the perfect phrase when you arrive at a restaurant with your family!
