💡 핵심 표현 Key Expressions
그러니까요
Exactly! / I know, right! / That's what I'm saying
A: 봄이 온 것 같아요.   B: 그러니까요!
A: It seems spring has come. B: I know, right!
하긴
Well / Come to think of it / You're right, though
하긴, 꽃샘 추위 때문인지 좀 춥긴 해요.
Well, it is a bit cold, maybe because of the cold snap.
아쉽네요
That's a shame / How disappointing / I'm a little sad about it
아쉽네요. 다음에 같이 가요!
What a shame. Let's go together next time!
~라 그런지
Maybe because it's ~ / Perhaps since it is ~
환절기라 그런지 감기도 유행이네요.
Maybe because it's the change of seasons, colds are going around.
💡 대화 배경: This is a workplace conversation (직장 대화) between colleagues on a spring morning. They discuss the changing season, plan lunch, and talk about the weather — very natural, everyday Korean! All dialogues end in polite ~요 form (존댓말).
💬 봄 대화 — Spring at the Office
📍 상황 (Situation): 봄날 아침, 직장 동료들의 대화 / Spring morning, colleagues at work
👨
민준
벌써 봄이 왔나 봐요. 출근길에 보니 개나리가 피고 있더라고요.
Beol-sseo bom-i wan-na bwa-yo. Chul-geun-gi-re bo-ni gae-na-ri-ga pi-go it-deo-ra-go-yo.
It seems spring has already come. On my way to work, I noticed forsythia was blooming.
🧑
지수
그러니까요. 날씨도 많이 따뜻해졌어요.
Geu-reo-ni-kka-yo. Nal-ssi-do ma-ni tta-tteu-tae-jyeo-sseo-yo.
Exactly. The weather has gotten much warmer too.
👩
수진
봄이 오니 봄소풍 가고 싶네요. 오늘 점심은 봄나물 어떠세요? 회사 앞에 비빔밥 맛집이 있어요.
Bo-mi o-ni bom-so-pung ga-go sim-ne-yo. O-neul jeom-si-meun bom-na-mul eo-tteo-se-yo? Hoe-sa ap-e bi-bim-bap mat-ji-bi it-seo-yo.
Now that spring is here, I want to go on a picnic. How about spring greens for lunch today? There's a great bibimbap restaurant in front of the office.
👱
현우
봄이라고 하지만, 아직 좀 추워요. 그냥 구내 식당에 가요. 거기도 비빔밥이 있어요.
Bom-i-ra-go ha-ji-man, a-jik jom chu-wo-yo. Geu-nyang gu-nae sik-dang-e ga-yo. Geo-gi-do bi-bim-bap-i it-seo-yo.
Even though they call it spring, it's still a bit cold. Let's just go to the cafeteria. They have bibimbap there too.
👨
민준
하긴, 꽃샘 추위 때문인지 좀 춥긴 해요.
Ha-gin, kkot-saem chu-wi ttae-mun-in-ji jom chup-gin hae-yo.
Well, it is a bit cold, maybe because of the late cold snap.
🧑
지수
네. 환절기라 그런지 감기도 유행이네요.
Ne. Hwan-jeol-gi-ra geu-reon-ji gam-gi-do yu-haeng-i-ne-yo.
Right. Maybe because it's the change of seasons, colds are going around.
👩
수진
아쉽네요. 그럼 날이 좀 더 따뜻해지면 꼭 나가요!
A-swim-ne-yo. Geu-reom na-ri jom deo tta-tteu-tae-ji-myeon kkok na-ga-yo!
That's a shame. Well, when it gets warmer, let's definitely go out!
📌 ~요 형식 안내: 이 대화의 모든 문장은 존댓말 ~요 형식으로 끝납니다. 원본 문장의 ~습니다 / ~갑시다 / ~합니다 형식을 모두 자연스러운 ~요 형식으로 바꿨어요. 하이라이트된 단어(보라색 배경)는 중요 단어와 문법 표현이에요!

💬 대화 퀴즈 Conversation Quiz

대화 내용을 얼마나 이해했나요? 8문제에 도전해 보세요!

Q1
민준이 출근길에 무엇을 봤나요? What did 민준 see on the way to work?
✅ "출근길에 보니 개나리가 피고 있더라고요." — He saw forsythia blooming on his commute.
Q2
그러니까요 는 어떤 의미인가요?
✅ 그러니까요 = strongly agreeing with the other person — like "Exactly!" or "I know, right!" It's the same as saying "That's what I was going to say!"
Q3
수진이 점심으로 제안한 것은? What did 수진 suggest for lunch?
✅ "오늘 점심은 봄나물 어떠세요? 회사 앞에 비빔밥 맛집이 있어요." — She suggested spring greens / bibimbap at a nearby restaurant.
Q4
현우가 구내 식당을 제안한 이유는? Why did 현우 suggest the cafeteria instead?
✅ "봄이라고 하지만, 아직 좀 추워요." — Even though it's spring, it's still cold. The grammar 봄이라고 하지만 = "even though they call it spring."
Q5
Fill in the blank: 하긴, 꽃샘 추위 _____ 좀 춥긴 해요. (Maybe because of the cold snap, it is a bit cold)
✅ 때문인지 = "maybe because of / perhaps due to." It expresses uncertain cause — "I don't know for sure, but maybe because of the cold snap..."
Q6
환절기라 그런지 의 뜻은?
✅ ~라 그런지 expresses an uncertain/soft reason — "maybe because it is ~." It's a natural way to suggest a possible cause without being definitive. Similar to ~때문인지.
Q7
마지막에 수진이 한 말의 의미는? What did 수진 say at the end?
✅ "아쉽네요. 그럼 날이 좀 더 따뜻해지면 꼭 나가요!" — What a shame, but let's definitely go out once it gets warmer. She's optimistic and looking forward to spring!
Q8
아쉽다 와 가장 비슷한 표현은?
✅ 아쉽다 = feeling disappointed or regretful about something that didn't go as hoped. "아쉽네요" is like saying "What a shame!" or "That's too bad!" — a very common Korean expression.
