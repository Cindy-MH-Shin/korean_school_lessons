🎉 보라의 생일 파티

Bora's Birthday Party

Page 1
👧🎂

오늘은 조카 보라의 생일이에요.

[o-neul-eun jo-ka bo-ra-ui saeng-il-i-e-yo]

Today is my niece Bora's birthday.

Page 2
💌🎈

나는 보라의 초대를 받았어요.

[na-neun bo-ra-reul cho-dae-bad-ass-eo-yo]

I was invited to Bora's party.

Page 3
🚶‍♀️🛣️

도로를 따라 걸어가다가 큰 로터리를 지났어요.

[do-ro-reul tta-ra geor-eo-ga-da-ga keun ro-teo-ri-reul ji-na-sseo-yo]

Walking along the road, I passed a big rotary.

Page 4
🚒🏢

로터리 옆에 빨간 소방서가 있었어요.

[ro-teo-ri yeop-e ppal-gan so-bang-seo-ga i-sseo-sseo-yo]

Next to the rotary was a red fire station.

Page 5
🏠🌸

보라네 집에 도착했어요.

[bo-ra-ne jib-e do-chak-hae-sseo-yo]

I arrived at Bora's house.

Page 6
🐰🐘

정원에서 토끼와 코끼리 인형이 나를 반겼어요.

[jeong-won-e-seo to-kki-wa ko-kki-ri in-hyeong-i na-reul ban-gyeo-sseo-yo]

In the garden, rabbit and elephant dolls greeted me.

Page 7
👧🎩

보라는 예쁜 모자를 쓰고 있었어요.

[bo-ra-neun ye-ppeun mo-ja-reul sseu-go i-sseo-sseo-yo]

Bora was wearing a pretty hat.

Page 8
🎵🎉

"생일 축하해, 보라야!" 나는 노래를 불렀어요.

[saeng-il chuk-ha-hae, bo-ra-ya! na-neun no-rae-reul bul-leo-sseo-yo]

"Happy birthday, Bora!" I sang a song.

Page 9
🍽️✨

식탁에는 맛있는 음식이 많았어요.

[sik-tak-e-neun ma-si-nneun eum-sik-i man-ass-eo-yo]

There was lots of delicious food on the table.

Page 10
🍠🥒🍇

고구마, 오이, 포도가 있었어요.

[go-gu-ma, o-i, po-do-ga i-sseo-sseo-yo]

There were sweet potatoes, cucumbers, and grapes.

Page 11
🎂🐯

호랑이 그림이 그려진 케이크도 있었어요!

[ho-rang-i geu-rim-i geu-ryeo-jin ke-i-keu-do i-sseo-sseo-yo!]

There was also a cake with a tiger drawing on it!

Page 12
😊🎈💕

우리는 함께 맛있는 음식을 먹고 즐거운 하루를 보냈어요.

[u-ri-neun ham-kke ma-si-nneun eum-sik-eul meok-go jeul-geo-un ha-ru-reul bo-nae-sseo-yo]

We ate delicious food together and had a wonderful day.

⭐ 끝 (The End) ⭐
